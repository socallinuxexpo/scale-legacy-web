Conference Speakers

The mission of the Southern California Linux Expo is education. To further that goal, each year the Southern California Linux Expo invites noted members of the Open Source Community to speak at SCALE.

Whether you are a beginner, a seasoned *NIX guru or just curious about Open Source, we have many worthwhile sessions for you to learn from this year!

Enjoy!
