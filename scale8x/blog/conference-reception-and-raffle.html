Conference Reception and Raffle

Submitted by ilan on Mon, 02/15/2010 - 17:03

We are excited to announce that the annual SCALE Conference Reception and Raffle are back for anothe year! Join us for refreshments hosted by  Google, Softlayer, Microlite, iXSystems, Oracle and Net2EZ.

While you enjoy a drink and mingle with other SCALE attendees, you will have a chance to win great raffle prizes.  

  • Books by Apress, Oreilly, Pearson, Packt Publishing, Wiley and other publishers.
  • 1yr SAGE Membership
  • Gear from CoolerMasters
  • HP Netbooks
  • Copies of Parallels Desktop for Linux
  • NetGear 802.11N Access Points
  • PogoLinux Workstation(s)
  • Marvell Plug Computers
  • PogoPlug
  • LogiTech Squeeze Box Radio
  • Firefox and Mozilla Schwag
  • T-shirts
  • and more...

And in keeping with our tradition of finding a new caffinated prize each year, we'll be giving away several cases of PerkyJerky.

We look forward to seeing you Saturday night at 9pm in the Concourse Ballroom!
