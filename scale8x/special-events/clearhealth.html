ClearHealth Training Event at SCALE 8x

ClearHealth Inc.  will be holding an intensive training event for their 3.0 Community Edition open source medical software on Saturday, Feb. 20th in the Logan conference room.The training will be split in two parts, beginning at 9:30 AM and concluding at 4:30. The first half will be a demonstration of features in 3.0 and the second half will cover technical details such as installation, server setup, and database overview.Attendees will also receive an early-access edition of the 3.1 source code. Advance tickets are available for purchase now, and SCALE registrants are eligible to purchase tickets the day of the event for $99. More information on the event, including ticket information, is available here.
