HotLava Systems

Exhibitor

HotLava Systems is a privately held company engaged in the design and manufacturing of innovative, server class multi-port Gigabit and 10-Gigabit Ethernet adapters. Founded in 2003 and based in Oakland, California, HotLava's customers include industry-leading innovators of network security solutions and Internet service providers throughout the world.

Our network adapters enable today's powerful servers and workstations to deliver more productivity by reducing or eliminating congestion at the network interface. Based on world class components and drivers from companies such as Intel, every HotLava network adapter card is natively supported by virtually all operating systems. They are ideally suited for space-limited network appliances and servers, providing the maximum port density and network bandwidth required by demanding I/O intensive applications.

With their high performance and low cost-per-port, HotLava products offer the best value in multi-port Gigabit and 10-Gigabit Ethernet network adapters.
