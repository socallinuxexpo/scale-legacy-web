Exhibitors

The Southern California Linux Expo features an exhibit floor for vendors to meet and interact with attendees. Past expositions have featured exhibits from companies such as IBM and Google, Linux user groups such as the San Gabriel Valley LUG, and non-profit organizations such as Linux Astronomy, KDE, and Fedora.

If your company (commercial organization) is interested in an exhibit booth, please contact the Sponsorship Committee for more information.

If your non-profit organization is interested in exhibiting, please contact The Community Relations Committee.
