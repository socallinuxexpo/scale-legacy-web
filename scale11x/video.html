Welcome to the 11th Annual Southern California Linux Expo!

Submitted by Ilan Rabinovitch on Sun, 2012-12-09 18:32

SCALE 11x will be held February 22-24, 2013 at the LAX Hilton.   SCALE 11X – the 11th annual Southern California Linux Expo – takes place on Feb. 22-24, 2013, at the Hilton Los Angeles Airport hotel. As the first-of-the-year Linux/Open Source software expo in North America, SCALE 11X expects to host more than 100 exhibitors this year, along with presenting more than 70 speakers. 

This year we will be turning your favorite open-conference up to 11 with an expanded set of co-located conferences and special events such as DevOps Day Los Angeles, the MySQL Community Conference, and many more.

Keynote Speakers

  • Matthew Garrett - The Secure Boot Journey
  • Kyle Rankin - Practical 3D Printing and the Open Source Community
