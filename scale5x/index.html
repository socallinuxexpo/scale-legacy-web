Welcome to the fifth annual

Southern California Linux Expo!

Held Saturday and Sunday, February 10-11, 2007 at The Westin Los Angeles Airport.

SCALE 5x offers expanded floor space. SCALE 5x promises to be everything you have come to expect from your Open Source community and much more.

SCALE 5x offers expanded floor space allowing for more for you to see and do, like an increased number of tutorial sessions designed to show users of all skill levels what Open Source can do and how to do it. In addition to expanded tutorials, SCALE 5x offers more booth space for those interested in showing how they have made Open Source work for them.

The booths are also a place to discover Open Source user groups in your area and meet and talk with some of the members. Whatever your interest in Open Source, SCALE 5x has something for you. For those staying over, The Westin is offering special hotel room rates. $99 / night with a maximum occupancy of 4.
