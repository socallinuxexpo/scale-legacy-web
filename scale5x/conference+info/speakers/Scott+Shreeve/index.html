Scott Shreeve, MD - Medsphere Systems Corporation
Co-founder
Speaking Topic: Value Driven Healthcare: The Convergence of Open Source with Healthcare IT

Dr. Shreeve is a board certified Emergency Medicine physician who has been actively involved in the design, development, and distribution of life science technologies that improve the delivery and enhance the quality of healthcare. Dr. Shreeve is a co-founder of Medsphere Systems Corporation, the first open source electronic health record for the healthcare enterprise. At Medsphere, he served as Chief Medical Officer in charge of the strategic focus, product marketing, and client services organization. Dr. Shreeve holds a B.S. degree in Zoology from Brigham Young University with a minor in Economics, is a graduate of the University of Utah School of Medicine, and completed his Emergency Medicine residency training at the University of Arizona Health Science Center. Dr. Shreeve lives in Aliso Viejo, California with his wife, two sons, and daughter.

  • Values - definition, defense of, societal importance, and directional impact
  • Open Source Values - definition, importance, and impact
  • Healthcare Values - definition, importance, impact, and examples
  • Convergence - OS and HIT, why this matters, what can result
  • Innovation - new models, the power of collaboration, community, etc
  • Future - Healthcare in the 21st century
