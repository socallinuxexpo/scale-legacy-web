Bob Cusick - Servoy
Co-founder and Managing Director of Servoy
Speaking Topic: Can RAD co-exist with SaaS, SOA, RIA and AJAX?

Bob Cusick is a co-founder of and the Managing Director of Servoy, USA. Previously, he founded ClickWare, Inc. a business application development and integration company; was the original technical editor for FileMaker Pro Advisor Magazine; was a founding member of the CSA (Claris Solutions Alliance), and was a recipient of the first (and only) "Claris Fellowship" award for outstanding service to that community. He has spoken at dozens of technology conferences around the globe, including Sydney, Melbourne, Amsterdam and of course, Silicon Valley. Bob is funny, outlandish and sometimes speaks a bit TOO much of the truth. You won.t want to miss his fast paced, action packed presentation.

Can you really do a rapid application development (RAD) project AND include SaaS (software as a service), SOA (software oriented architecture) and deploy it as a Rich Internet App (RIA) or AJAX improved web application? With Servoy you can. And Servoy is OS agnostic. It can run on Linux, as well as PC or Mac systems. Come find out in this fun, fast paced session how you can create web and business applications quickly for the Linux OS utilizing this tool.

Servoy is a development and deployment environment that has been proven to be the fastest and easiest way to build and deploy rich business internet applications (RIA) and/or enhance legacy applications. With Servoy you can develop and deploy a GUI database application in minutes. Servoy uses drag-and-drop, automatic data-binding, and a built-in script library
