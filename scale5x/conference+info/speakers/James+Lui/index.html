James Lui - Ericom
Director of Technology
Speaking Topic: Linux Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI) Access

James Lui is the Director of Technology for Ericom Software, a leading provider of enterprise-wide application access solutions. With over 10 years of experience within the IT industry, James leads numerous initiatives that require implementation of the latest technology platforms.

As a platform-agnostic solution, PowerTerm WebConnect provides secure local and remote access to both virtual Linux desktops and Windows desktops running on virtual servers such as VMware GSX and ESX. The primary difference of virtualized Linux desktops vs. NoMachine or LTSP Linux Terminal Server is that with virtualized desktops - users can access their own full desktop OS which is not shared with other users. This increases security (totally private OS), flexibility for business continuity and provides additional benefits. The presentation will include a short live demo.
