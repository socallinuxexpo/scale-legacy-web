Birds of a Feather

Linux Expo of SoCal would like to host your BoF at the Fifth Annual So Cal Linux Expo February 10th & 11th, 2007. The BoF sessions will be 45 minutes to 1 hour long.

Deadline for Submissions: February 2nd, 2007

February 10th: Birds of a Feather Sessions

Time Exhibit Hall Midway Logan La Guardia Kennedy
7:00pm-8:00pm Closed Building a Linux Home Entertainment System Features of Inkscape Linux on Consumer Devices Asterisk, TrixBox and OpenPBX
8:00pm-9:00pm Closed Introducing the Haiku Operating System OpenNMS, what is it good for? Ubuntu Enterprise level MySQL

February 11th: Birds of a Feather Sessions

Time Exhibit Hall Midway Logan La Guardia Kennedy
7:00pm-8:00pm Closed Reserved A New Kind of Robotics Challenge with Squeak Schools and Open Source Software Haiku Technology Insight
8:00pm-9:00pm Closed Small & Regional LUG Networking Statewide Community Technology Roadmap Gentoo OpenSolaris


If you'd like to organize a BoF at SCALE, the Expo will publicize it and host it for you.

Please send the following info to g at socallinuxexpo.org
  • Title of the BoF
  • Brief description of BoF topic
  • Group hosting the BoF
  • Contact person for BoF (discussion leader/moderator,etc)
