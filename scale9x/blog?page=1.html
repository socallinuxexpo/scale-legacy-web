SCALE Moves Up!

Submitted by orv on Jun 24, 2010

In order to accommodate its steady growth over the past few years, the Southern California Linux Expo (SCALE) will move to a new home in the Hilton Los Angeles Airport, starting with SCALE 9X in 2011.

The new venue – larger than SCALE’s former home for the last four years at the Westin Los Angeles Airport – will allow SCALE to expand its offerings at SCALE 9X, slated for Feb. 25-27, 2011.
