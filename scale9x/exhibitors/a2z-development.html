A2Z Development

Reception Sponsor

A2Z Research and Development, Inc. is a wholly owned subsidiary of Amazon.com. We have a fast growing collaborative environment with the agility and focus of a startup. We’re an agile group so we emphasize best practices and personal discipline instead of heavyweight formal processes. Our Orange County, CA office is hard at work on numerous exciting and innovative products in the mobile, games and interactive media technology space.

 

Do you love to code? So do we! Are you looking for a place that believes in you? That’s us! Are you excited about extending the game and interactive media experience in new and creative ways? That’s our mission! Have you ever thought about the work involved in building products and services that grow to the scale that Amazon requires. That’s what makes it fun!

 

Our cutting-edge team recently made a big splash in the mobile space with the launch of the Amazon Appstore for Android Developer Portal. The Amazon Appstore storefront is set to launch this year and we’re looking for the best of the best to help us achieve it! We are also expanding our investment in existing projects and adding several new teams. These are very cool projects – you’ll be working on things that are still on the drawing board or that you invent. This is a place for people who want to be involved and who want to help navigate. We’d love to tell you more, but you’ll have to come in and talk to us first!

 

To find out more about the next step in your career, please email your resume to jobs-a2zoc@amazon.com and we will contact you with more detail.
