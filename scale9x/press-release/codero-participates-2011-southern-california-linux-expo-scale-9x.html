Codero Participates in 2011 Southern California Linux Expo (SCALE 9x)

Feb 15, 2011

Lenexa, KA – Codero will be at the Southern California Linux Expo (SCALE) 9X-2011 February 25 through February 27, 2011 at the Hilton Los Angeles Airport Hotel to discuss how to optimize an existing infrastructure solution as well as the company’s upcoming launch of cloud hosting offering 24/7/365 support and a 99.9% Uptime Guarantee.  SCALE is a community run Linux and open-source conference now in its ninth year.

This year, SCALE features five speaker tracks for the growing population of attendees interested in using open source alternatives for system administration tasks. The conference is in sync with Codero’s initiatives to reach out to the open source community. Codero provides optimized infrastructure to customers in the e-commerce, Saas, web development, advertising and media sectors and now offers a new cloud beta platform.

One of the keynote speakers for this year’s SCALE event is Canonical Chief Executive Officer Jane Silber who will expound on the topic, “Cloud for Human Beings.” The event will host more than 80 exhibitors and 60 speakers and presenters.

About Codero
A leading provider of optimized hosting infrastructure, Codero creates flexible, scalable solutions using dedicated and managed hosting. All Codero services and products are backed by SAS 70 Type II secure data centers, live 24/7/365 U.S.-based support and a seasoned staff with international experience in the hosting business for over 16 years. An industry innovator, Codero has a customer loyalty Rewards Program and offers green hosting from the Phoenix, Arizona data center and four points of presence. For more information about Codero, please visit www.codero.com.
