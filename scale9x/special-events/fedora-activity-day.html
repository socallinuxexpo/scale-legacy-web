Fedora Activity Day

When: 
Friday, February 25, 2011 - 09:00 - 17:00


The Fedora Project hosts its annual Fedora Activity Day at SCALE 9x on Friday, February 25, beginning at 9 a.m.

Fedora Activity Day will focus on building community resources to educate Fedora community members about System Administration.  This study session is to help those pursuing additional goals, including higher-pay, certifications and much more.
