SCALE University

When: 
Friday, February 25, 2011 - 09:00 - 17:00

The SCALE University training program continues for SCALE 9x. It is presented by the League of Professional System Administrators (LOPSA) in partnership with SCALE. The classes give a more intense and personal learning experience than a 45-minute seminar.

We are offering four half-day classes for system administrators of all skill and experience levels. The SCALE University Pass includes a full day of training (two classes of your choice) and full access to all three days of SCALE. To register, select the "SCALE 9x Full Access Pass" and then the "SCALE University Pass" which will include a $60 discount on your total charge.

The SCALE University classes will be held on Friday, February 25th, 2011.

Schedule: 

Morning Sessions (9am -  12:30pm):

  • Introduction to Automating System Administration with Cfengine (Aleksey Tsalolikhin)
  • Security Best Practices and Tools for Linux (Matt Disney)

    •

Afternoon Sessions (1:30pm -  5:30pm):

  • Application Acceleration from a Data Storage Perspective (Sam Eckhouse)
  • BackupPC: Enterprise, Cross Platform Backups for Everyone  (Ski Kacoroski)
