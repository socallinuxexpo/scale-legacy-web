Welcome to the sixth annual

Southern California Linux Expo!

The Southern California Linux Expo was Friday, Saturday and Sunday, February 8-10, 2008 at the Westin Los Angeles Airport.

SCALE 6x was a success! Over 1400 registrants visited SCALE over three days, viewing 80 booths and sitting in on dozens of talks. The press and bloggers have been uniformly enthusiastic about SCALE. See the Media tab for a sample of those reviews.

Many of the presentations are now available. See the schedule or the speaker information to view them.
