Women In Open Source

The SCALE 6X Women In Open Source conference continues our past efforts to encourage women of all ages to be a part of the Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) community. We invite you to showcase your work on Free and Open Source projects. Join us in sharing your accomplishments, success stories, and advancements.

Past attendees at this event have included women in technology, teachers, and parents of young girls.

Open Source Software in Education

The So Cal Linux Expo is proud to announce their first Education miniconference. The Education miniconference will be held on February 8th. Continuing our efforts to promote open source, we invite you to share your success in employing Free and Open Source Software in education with the rest of the community at this conference.

Demonstrating Open-Source Healthcare Solutions (DOHCS) at SCALE 6X

The Southern California Linux Expo is proud to announce the second "Demonstrating Open Source Health Care Solutions" (DOHCS) Open Source health care conference. The conference will be held on February 8, 2008, prior to the 6th Annual So Cal Linux expo.

We invite you to share your work with or use of Open Source health care software with the rest of the community, and exchange ideas with some of the leading experts in these fields.

In response to the overwhelming demand from our 2007 event, the conference will be expanded to offer 2 session tracks. One track will focus on the technical aspects of operating in the complex environment of health care IT, and the other on the business aspects, case studies, and open source successes throughout health care as a whole.
