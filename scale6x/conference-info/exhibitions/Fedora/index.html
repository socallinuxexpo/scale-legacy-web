Fedora

What is Fedora? Fedora is a set of projects sponsored by Red Hat and guided by the Fedora contributors. These projects are developed by a large community of people who strive to provide and maintain the very best in free, open source software and standards. Fedora, the central Fedora project, is an operating system and platform based on Linux which is free for anyone to use, modify, and distribute, now and forever.

The operating system is Fedora. It's updated approximately twice a year. It's completely free, and the Fedora group is committed to keeping it that way. It's the best combination of the most robust and latest software that exists in the free software world.

Visit website for more information: http://www.fedoraproject.org
